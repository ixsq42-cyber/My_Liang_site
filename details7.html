Product Description

Upgrade your equipment with these heavy-duty caster wheels. Designed for durability and smooth operation, these casters are perfect for industrial, medical, and home use.

  • HEAVY DUTY: Made of heavy duty steel and polyurethane wheels.
  • DUAL LOCKING: The total lock brake locks both the wheel and the swivel simultaneously.
  • QUIET MOVEMENT: Non-marking polyurethane wheels roll smoothly and silently.

Installation Guide

1. Ensure mounting surface is flat and clean.

2. Use 5/16" bolts (not included) to secure the top plate.

3. Tighten all 4 bolts securely using a wrench.

4. Check stability before applying full load.

Warranty Information

This product comes with a 1-Year Limited Warranty covering manufacturing defects. If you experience any issues with the locking mechanism or wheel degradation under normal use, please contact our support team.

Safety Warnings

WARNING: Do not exceed the recommended load capacity of 600 lbs per caster. Overloading may cause failure and injury. Ensure brakes are engaged when equipment is stationary on a slope.
